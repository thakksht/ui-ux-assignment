Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Information

What is TechFest 2025?

TechFest 2025 is an annual technical festival organized by Tech University, featuring programming competitions, robotics challenges, design contests, and gaming tournaments. It's a 3-day event bringing together students from across the country to compete, learn, and network.

When and where is TechFest 2025 being held?

TechFest 2025 will be held from March 15-17, 2025, at Tech University Main Campus, New Delhi. The main venue is the Auditorium Complex with various events happening across different departments.

Who can participate in TechFest 2025?

All undergraduate and postgraduate students from recognized universities and colleges can participate. Some events may have specific eligibility criteria mentioned in their individual descriptions.

Is there an age limit for participation?

Participants must be currently enrolled students in undergraduate or postgraduate programs. There's no specific age limit, but valid student ID is required during registration.

Registration & Payment

How much is the registration fee?

The registration fee is ₹200 per participant, which includes access to all competitions, participant kit, meals during the event, certificate, and event T-shirt.

When is the registration deadline?

Registration closes on March 10, 2025, at 11:59 PM. We recommend registering early as some events have limited seats.

Can I register for multiple events?

Yes, you can register for multiple events. However, please check the schedule to ensure there are no timing conflicts between your chosen events.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI payments, bank transfers, and online payment gateways. Payment details will be provided after form submission.

Is the registration fee refundable?

Registration fees are non-refundable after March 12, 2025. However, refunds may be considered for genuine emergencies with proper documentation.

Events & Competitions

What programming languages are allowed in coding competitions?

For most programming events, we support C++, Java, Python, and JavaScript. Specific requirements for each event are listed on the Events page.

Do I need to bring my own laptop for competitions?

Yes, participants should bring their own laptops for most events. For some specific competitions, systems may be provided - this will be mentioned in the event guidelines.

Can I participate in team events if I don't have a team?

Yes! We have a team formation forum on our website and Discord channel where individual participants can find teammates. We'll also facilitate team formation during registration.

Are there any software requirements for design events?

For design competitions, you can use Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch, Photoshop, or any professional design software you're comfortable with. Specific requirements are listed for each event.

Logistics & Accommodation

Is accommodation provided for outstation participants?

We can help arrange accommodation in nearby hostels and budget hotels. Please mention your accommodation requirements in the registration form, and we'll provide a list of recommended places.

Are meals included in the registration fee?

Yes, the registration fee includes breakfast, lunch, and evening snacks for all three days of the event.

How do I reach the venue?

Tech University is well-connected by metro, bus, and taxi. The nearest metro station is University Line - Tech Campus (2 minutes walk). Detailed directions will be provided after registration.

Is parking available at the venue?

Yes, free parking is available for participants. However, spaces are limited, so we recommend using public transportation when possible.

Prizes & Certificates

What prizes are offered for competitions?

We offer cash prizes totaling ₹50,000 across all events, plus trophies, certificates, and internship opportunities. Prize details for each event are listed on the Events page.

Will all participants receive certificates?

Yes, all registered participants will receive certificates of participation. Winners will receive additional certificates of achievement.

When will prizes be distributed?

Prize distribution ceremony will be held on March 17, 2025, at 6:00 PM during the closing ceremony. Winners must be present to collect their prizes.

Technical Support

What if I face technical issues during online registration?

Contact our technical support team at tech-support@techfest2025.edu or call +91-9876543212. We also have live chat support available during office hours.

Will internet connectivity be provided during the event?

Yes, high-speed Wi-Fi will be available throughout the venue. Network credentials will be provided during check-in.

Is there technical support available during competitions?

Yes, we have dedicated technical support volunteers available during all competitions to help with any hardware or software issues.
